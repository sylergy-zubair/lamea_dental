# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Adults 25-45 seeking cosmetic dental work (gaps, chips, discoloration). Mobile-first, 90%+ Meta ad traffic. London/UK market. Price-conscious but quality-focused. Want confidence, not a sales pitch.

## Product Purpose

Lamea Dental is a cosmetic dental clinic website. Primary goal: convert ad traffic into leads via AI Smile Preview and WhatsApp booking. Success =20%+ lead conversion, <2s load, 3-4x WhatsApp bookings vs forms.

## Brand Personality

Premium, warm, confident. Non-salesy. "Elegant simplicity" — the Apple of dental. Clean without being cold. Trust through transparency.

## Anti-references

- NOT blue/turquoise dental clichés
- NOT gold/yellow "premium" dental
- NOT navy "clinical" feel
- NOT aggressive sales copy or countdown timers
- NOT overly decorated (no stock photo clutter, no gradient overload)

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell** — Let results speak. Before/after beats promises.
2. **Transparent pricing** — Ranges shown upfront. No bait-and-switch.
3. **Expert confidence** — Not自负, just quietly assured. The work is the hero.
4. **Warmth without kitsch** — Approachable but never childish or playful.
5. **Speed as feature** — <2s load. Fast interactions signal quality.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA contrast
- Mobile-first (90%+ mobile traffic)
- Reduced motion support
- No autoplay audio
